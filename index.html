The Poo Poo Crab Song

There was a poo poo crab in the bottom of the great Pacific deep-blue sea

She had many friends,just like you and me...

An Octopus...

A Sea Turtle...

And a Sea Anenome...

And one morning that Poo Poo Crab said outloud,

"I'm Just tired of eating

Plankton

Brine Shrimp

And Sea Monkeys Too

I just want something new"

So she marcherd her way...

To the Dunes of Choongaloo

Where she waited and waited and waited

At first she could smell it

And then she could see it

In the corner of her eye

It was round and brown and about to float by

So she opened up her claw nice and wide

Pinched it down on the poo poo

Popped it in her mouth, chewed and chewed and said,

"mmm-mmmmm

I'm the Poo Poo Crab of the Great Pacific deep-blue sea

But now I must get some sleep"

So she laid her head down

On the kelp bed

Among the sea-weed.
